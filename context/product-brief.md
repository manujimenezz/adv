# **PRD - Website**

## **Advin Capital**

Version: 1.0  
Type: Corporate Institutional Website  
Objective: Position Advin Capital as an independent investment advisory firm specialized in structured real asset transactions.

# **1\. Project Overview**

### **Purpose**

Create a minimal, institutional corporate website that positions **Advin Capital** as a structured investment advisory boutique operating in hospitality and real assets.

The website should convey:

- Institutional credibility
- Discretion
- Structured transaction capability
- International scope

The website **must not resemble a real estate agency**.

No property listings.  
No promotional marketing language.

Tone:  
Investment boutique / financial advisory.

# **2\. Target Audience**

Primary audience:

- Institutional investors
- Family offices
- Private equity funds
- Hotel investors
- Real estate developers
- Asset owners

Secondary audience:

- Strategic partners
- Operators
- Advisors
- Legal and financial professionals

# **3\. Brand Positioning**

Advin Capital is positioned as:

**Independent Investment Advisory & Capital Structuring Firm**

Focus sectors:

- Hospitality
- Real assets
- Structured transactions

The brand should communicate:

- Discretion
- Precision
- Institutional rigor
- Strategic thinking

# **4\. Website Structure**

Total pages: **4-5**

- Home
- Firm
- Advisory
- Approach (Methodology)
- Contact
